# Article Origins

Every post and generation job stores one origin: `autopilot`, `trend_autopilot`, `admin_manual`, `admin_custom_headline`, `admin_batch`, `editor_update`, or `scheduled_manual`.

Only `autopilot` and `trend_autopilot` consume automatic daily and weekly quotas. Manual articles, custom headlines, manual batches, and updates have separate counters. They remain subject to authentication, rate, batch, concurrency, and provider limits.
