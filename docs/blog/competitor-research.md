# Competitor Research

Administrator-supplied public competitor pages are fetched by the worker using the same network protections as other editorial sources. SEOIntel stores compact title, heading-theme, topic-gap, and format observations, not raw page HTML.

The resulting brief identifies covered topics and unanswered questions. It explicitly prohibits copying wording, examples, tables, paragraph order, or heading sequence. When analytics are unavailable, the record says `traffic data unavailable`.
